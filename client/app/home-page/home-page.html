About / How to play?


Tambola / Housie

As a rule, each player must buy at least one ticket to enter a game. A typical 90 ball Tambola ticket consists of 3 rows and 9 columns which make 27 spaces. Each row has a total of 5 numbers printed on it. A column can have 1, 2 or the maximum 3 numbers printed on it. The first column in the ticket will have numbers from 1-9, the second column will have 10-19, third column with 20-29 and so on until the 9th column which will be numbered in between 80-90



Gameplay

The game begins with a ball draw. As the game progresses, the board is marked with each ball that is drawn. The objective of the game is to mark/ dab all the numbers found in the ticket as called by the dealer. The player who first mark all the numbers in a winning pattern and calls a win is declared as the WINNER of that pattern after the dealer checks his ticket and verify it with numbers drawn. If your claimed winning pattern is wrong, it will be called BOOGY and you cannot continue the game with the same ticket. The game ends when all 90 numbers are drawn, or when a winner is declared for all the patterns of the game, whichever comes first.



Winning patterns

In order to win in Tambola or Bingo, you need to match winning combinations. Some of the popular winning combinations in a Tambola game are given below:

  • Early Five : The ticket with first five number dabbed
  • Top Line: The ticket with all the numbers of the top row dabbed fastest.
  • Middle Line: The ticket with all the numbers of the middle row dabbed fastest.
  • Bottom Line: The ticket with the numbers of the bottom row dabbed fasted.
  • Four Corners: The ticket with all four corners marked first i.e. 1st and last numbers of top and bottom rows.
  • Full House: The ticket with all the 15 numbers marked first.



Using the site


Login

You need to be logged in to play this game of Tambola. Visit the Login page to sign-in via multiple options. i.e Facebook, Google or Twitter



Host / Join game

After signing-in you can either create a new game as a host and invite other people to you game. Or you can join games that you have been invited to. The join option takes you to another screen where you can enter the code shared with you by the host. Enter the code and you are all set!



Projector view

This is the view that you will be logged into by default. It is there to keep track of called numbers and to see if anyone is saying anything about you! You can see the status of which rules have been claimed and which remain. You will also be seeing if anyone claimed anything and whether he/she was able to claim it.



Ticket view

This will be the view where you will be checking off the numbers being called. You can claim the rules from here easily. You can also send messages for everyone in the game. These are volatile messages and are not stored for future. You can also see how many lives you have remaining by watching the s on top of your screen
